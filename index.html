About Me

Growing up with almost unrestricted internet access has meant the internet has shaped who I am and what I create in immeasurable ways. As I got older, I discovered post-internet art and began to realise the untouched potential the internet holds when it comes to the fields of art. My first foray into post internet art was a photography portfolio I did for a level 3 external. The photos hosted on this site created for said portfolio. I hope this collection can inspire and awaken something in you the same way post-internet art did for me.
